## Brooklyn Wellness Studio Application

### Tell us about your work and who you serve
I am the founder of Let Me Say It For You LLC, a multi-divisional wellness studio offering mindfulness practices, somatic healing, and creative cultural programming. Through my signature practice P.A.U.S.E. (Patience, Accountability, Uniting, Self, Experience), I guide individuals and communities in cultivating stillness, balance, and authentic presence. I serve diverse communities with an emphasis on Black and Afro-diasporic populations, lifestyle collectives, and wellness seekers who value holistic healing that is both culturally relevant and transformative.

### Top 3 Strengths
1. **Innovative Healing Practices** — blending mindfulness, somatic movement, and cultural traditions
2. **Community Building** — connecting with audiences, curating safe spaces, fostering relationships
3. **Creative Program Design** — crafting immersive experiences that nourish mind, body, and spirit

### Skills & Offerings
- P.A.U.S.E. Wellness Workshops (breathwork, meditation, somatic healing)
- Bound & Beyond sessions (conscious movement and body liberation)
- Nutrition Nebula mindful cooking and wellness food experiences
- Sound meditation and breath-focused healing practices
- Proven experience curating wellness activations across NYC

### Closing Statement
I bring a multidisciplinary approach that merges wellness, artistry, and cultural education. My work emphasizes the sacred balance of mental health and creativity, offering participants spaces to feel safe, empowered, and deeply seen.
