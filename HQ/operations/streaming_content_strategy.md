## Thumbnail Optimization Tips

### Must-Haves
- High contrast colors
- Clear emotional facial expression
- Readable text at 20% size
- Focal points in 16:9 safe zone

### Text Hierarchy
- Keep speech bubbles 10-15% smaller
- Add subtle gradient for depth
- Include your brand watermark

### A/B Testing
- Version A: Provocative hook
- Version B: Introspective/wellness angle
