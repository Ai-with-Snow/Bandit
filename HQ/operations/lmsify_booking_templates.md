## Intake/Consent Form Prompts

### Pre-Session Intake Questions

1. What is your intention for this session?
2. Is there anything happening in your body or life that I should know about?
3. Are there any touch boundaries or sensory preferences? (e.g., no face touch, prefer lighter pressure)
4. Have you experienced somatic work before?
5. What does aftercare look like for you?

### Consent Statement
*By booking this session, you confirm that you are participating voluntarily, understand this is a wellness/education experience (not therapy), and have communicated any boundaries.*
