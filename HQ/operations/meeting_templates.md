- Gratitude pause

## 15-Minute Sprint Meeting

When time is tight:

| Time | Focus |
|------|-------|
| 0:00 - 2:00 | Grounding Start |
| 2:00 - 6:00 | Reflections (highlight + challenge) |
| 6:00 - 10:00 | Active Tasks (status + next step) |
| 10:00 - 14:00 | Next Event Focus + assignments |
| 14:00 - 15:00 | Closing + gratitude |

- Pin agenda in Discord meeting channel before call

- Color-code by division for clarity
- Weekly or bi-weekly based on workload
- End on energy, not stress
