# Velvet Evenings & Velvet Hours Dossier

Source: Chat thread on branding the after-work P.A.U.S.E.

## Experience Overview
- **Primary Title:** Velvet Evenings — An After Work P.A.U.S.E.
- **Extended Title (retreat upgrade):** Velvet Hours — The 2-Hour P.A.U.S.E. Retreat (optionally extended to a 3-hour experience).
- **Core Tagline:** “Softness meets luxury at the end of your grind.”
- **Alt Taglines:** “Slip into stillness. Indulge in the reset.” / “Stretch time. Indulge in stillness.”
- **Vibe:** Moody, sensual, luxury calm. Ambient beats, incense, velvet lighting, conversation-friendly but guided.
- **Brand Position:** “Not another class—this is a 2-hour luxury ritual that transforms stress into stillness.”

## 2-Hour Flow (Velvet Evenings)
| Segment | Duration | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Arrival & Transition | 15 min | Velvet-lit space, incense, Afrocentric lounge beats, ritual to “drop” the workday. |
| Somatic Unwind | 25 min | Guided stretch, posture release, self/partner massage prompts. |
| Breath & Stillness | 20 min | Intentional breath cycles, grounding meditation with sultry soundscape. |
| Creative Indulgence | 20 min | Journaling prompts, coloring/art cards, optional partner reflection. |
| Sound & Sensation Immersion | 30 min | Sound bowls, rain sticks, textured props, body scan + sensory cues. |
| Nourishment & Integration | 20 min | Tea or Nutrition Nebula bites, intention sharing, send-off ritual. |

## Pricing Architecture
### Audience Tiers
- **Community / Local Spaces:** $35–$55 per person (or $400–$600 for 15–20 people).
- **Corporate / Professional Groups:** $85–$125 per person or $1,500–$2,500 for 20–25 employees. Upsells include Velvet Journals, branded treats, meditation recordings.
- **Lifestyle / Intimate Circles:** $65–$100 per person or $900–$1,800 flat rate.
- **Private Luxury Groups:** $250–$500 per person or $1,200–$3,000 for 4–8 people with gift kits/travel add-ons.

### Shopify Ticketing Strategy
- **Community Access:** $33.33 (angel-number pricing, cap at 5–7 seats).
- **Standard Admission:** $77 (headline rate; even 10 tickets covers costs and margin).
- **VIP Velvet Ticket:** $111 (reserved spots + token bundle, incense, or journal).
- **Add-On Merch:** Life Token bundles ($22), Velvet Journal + Incense kit ($33), Deluxe token kit ($55). Upsell at checkout or in person to double revenue while keeping entry accessible.

### Cost Considerations
- Venue rental baseline: $180. Break-even at ~6 seats when priced at $33.33; recommended tiers protect profit while retaining accessibility.

## Flyers & Visual Assets
- **Sensual velvet flyers** targeted at guests with moody textures, minimal copy, and highlight itinerary.
- **Autumn palette version:** deep velvet background with golden/copper fonts, fewer words, 3-hour mention.
- **Royal eggplant version:** high-contrast purple velvet background for premium retreat iterations.
- **Key copy to include:** Title, tagline, bullet highlights (Somatic Unwind · Breath & Stillness · Sound Immersion · Creative Indulgence · Closing Nourishment), optional itinerary callouts.
- **Ticket template:** Visual pass referencing flyer art, includes event title, date/time, attendee name field, and QR/stamp zone.

## Pitch Notes (Community + 420-Friendly Crowd)
- **Hook:** “You’ve talked and smoked after work—where’s the space to actually drop into your body?”  
- **Script:** “Velvet Hours is a two-hour after-work decompression ritual. Think grounding, conversation, subtle movement, sound healing, and snacks. It’s moody, 420-friendly, and built to reset your whole vibe before the night starts.”
- **Sensory Props:** Carry incense/token samples during outreach so the vibe feels tangible immediately.
