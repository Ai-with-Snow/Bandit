# P.A.U.S.E. Classes & Workshop Pillars

Source: Goddexx Snow × Bandit chat transcript (Velvet Evenings + PleasHer planning).

## Pillars of Focus
- **MindFULLness & Stillness Training** — breathwork, meditation, grounding, and the core P.A.U.S.E. acronym woven into every session.
- **Somatic Flow & Body Connection** — dance, yoga, posture reset, stretch, touch, and partner trust drills to reconnect body + mind.
- **Cultural Nourishment & Afrocentric Wellness** — food rituals (Nutrition Nebula), Rastafarian/Caribbean/Black healing traditions, storytelling, sound, and drumming.
- **Creative Expression & Healing** — journaling, art, coloring, music, life tokens, and sensory play for emotional release.
- **Sacred Intimacy & Connection** — consent-led pleasure pedagogy, wax play demos, body-positive intimacy healing (for curated audiences).

## Catalog Additions
### 1. The Art of PleasHer — Sacred Sensuality Course
- Focus: Teaches men/masculine energies to honor and pleasure women through presence, consent, and sacred touch.
- Elements: Breathwork, touch techniques, communication scripts, body literacy, optional wax play demos.
- Duration: Expandable from 90 mins to multi-week series.
- Vibe: Afrocentric, intimate, healing, reverent.

### 2. After Work P.A.U.S.E. → Velvet Evenings / Velvet Hours
- Focus: Luxury decompression ritual for professionals who want to unwind, feel sensual-calm, and reset after the grind.
- Elements: Somatic unwind, breathwork, creative indulgence, sound bath, nourishment.
- Duration: Matured from 75 mins to 2-hour (Velvet Evenings) and optional 3-hour retreat concepts.
- Vibe: Moody, velvet, sensual, conversation-friendly; accessible for corporate and lifestyle spaces.
