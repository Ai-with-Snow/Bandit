# P.A.U.S.E. Portal Vetting Form Notes

Source: Chat instructions referencing Irie Igloo vetting blueprint.

## Form Sections
1. **Basic Information**
   - Full Name / Preferred Name
   - Pronouns
   - Age confirmation (18+)
   - Contact email
   - Referral source (how they heard about the portal)
2. **Connection to P.A.U.S.E.**
   - What draws you to the portal?
   - Previous mindfulness/wellness/community experience (Y/N + describe).
   - Current practices (meditation, somatic, mindfulness).
3. **Boundaries, Consent & Safety**
   - “What does consent mean to you?”
   - “How do you communicate boundaries?”
   - “If someone’s boundary is crossed, what do you do?”
   - Agreement to community guidelines (no harassment, respect identities).
4. **Values & Identity (Optional)**
   - Alignment with centered communities (Black, queer, trans, POC, etc.).
   - Values the participant brings to mindful, healing space.
5. **Practical Considerations**
   - Accessibility / support needs.
   - Agreement to safety practices (incl. COVID if applicable).
   - Emergency contact (optional but encouraged).
6. **Final Reflection**
   - “What do you hope to gain from this portal?”
   - Open field for additional notes.

## Disclaimer (Energy Liberation & Sensual Kundalini Strengthening)
> Participation may involve practices that awaken energy flow, emotional release, and sensual kundalini strengthening. Experiences are designed for healing and self-connection but may trigger strong sensations or emotions. By entering, participants acknowledge they are responsible for honoring personal boundaries, may pause/leave at any time, and that facilitators hold space with consent and mindfulness. This is not medical or mental health care; consult trusted professionals if needed. Signature + Date required to confirm understanding and engagement with respect.
