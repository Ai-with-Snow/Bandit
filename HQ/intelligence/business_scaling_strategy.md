## LLC Structure for Acquisition

### 1. Parent LLC (Holding Company)
**Let Me Say It For You LLC (LMSIFY)**
- Owns all divisions and future acquisitions
- Single EIN
- Applies for SBA loans

### 2. Divisions as DBAs
- SnowDayKokoa (DBA)
- Mix Up Mash Up Kitchen (DBA)
- Life Tokens Shop (DBA)
- P.A.U.S.E. (DBA)

### 3. Subsidiary LLCs for Acquisitions
When buying a business:
- Create: **LMSIFY Acquisitions LLC**
- Protects parent brand from risk
- Clean bookkeeping for SBA

### Requirements for SBA Readiness
- Clean financials
- LLC properly filed
- Business bank account (separate)
- Decent credit
- Proof of cash flow or business plan
- NO co-mingled personal/business spending

## SBA 7(a) Loan Strategy

### What It Is
- Government-backed business loan
- 10% down payment
- 10-25 year repayment
- Low interest rates
- Can buy entire businesses, equipment, real estate

### The Play
1. Find profitable business (e.g., laundromat making $200K/year)
2. Get SBA loan for 90% of cost
3. Pay 10% down
4. Business cash flow pays the loan
5. Profit from excess
6. Own asset you barely paid for upfront

## Notion Organization Tips

### Four Core Databases
1. **Business Operations** â€” Projects, events, workshops, sessions
2. **Content Hub** â€” Posts, captions, media planning
3. **Financials** â€” Revenue, expenses, goals, event logs

### Structure Rules

- Create views (Calendar, Board, Table, Gallery)
- Use templates inside each database
- Use toggles to hide long sections
- Pin only 2 pages in sidebar
- Build only what you're actively moving
