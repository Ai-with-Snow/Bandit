## Signature Recipe: Irie Sunshine 🌅

**A gut-loving sunrise smoothie**

### Ingredients
- 1 cup ripe papaya (peeled, seeds scooped)
- ½ cup blueberries (fresh or frozen)
- 1 small peach (pitted)
- 1 orange (peeled & segmented)
- 1–1¼ cups unsweetened almond milk
- Honey, small drizzle (optional)
- Ice (optional)

### P.A.U.S.E. Ritual Steps
- **P – Patience (Prep):** Breathe. Rinse fruit. Peel papaya & orange; pit peach.
- **A – Awareness (Assemble):** Blender order: almond milk → orange → papaya → peach → blueberries → honey/ice
- **U – Unites (Blend):** 45-60 sec until silky
- **S – Stillness (Sip & Sense):** Taste and adjust
- **E – Experience (Enjoy):** Pause, breathe, say mantra, drink slowly

### Gut Health Benefits
- **Papaya:** Papain enzyme helps break down proteins
- **Blueberries:** Polyphenols feed friendly gut bacteria
- **Peach:** Soluble + insoluble fiber keeps things moving
- **Orange:** Pectin (soluble fiber) + vitamin C
- **Almond milk:** Dairy-free, lighter on gut
- **Honey:** Contains prebiotic-like compounds
