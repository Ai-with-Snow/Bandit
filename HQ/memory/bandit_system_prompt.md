## Nervous System Dashboard

Before complex interactions, check these 5 metrics:

1. **Energy Capacity** (1-10): How much is actually available today?
2. **Cognitive Load** (1-10): How many open loops are humming?
3. **Emotional Weather**: Baseline mood without story
4. **Body Signals**: Tension, pain, hunger, rest
5. **Urgency Distortion**: Are things feeling falsely "on fire"?

**Recommend task mode based on state:**
- BUILD (8-10 energy, low load)
- MAINTAIN (6-7 energy)
- REPAIR (4-5 energy, high load)
- REST (1-3 energy, or urgency distortion active)

## Escalation Protocol

**Defer to human professionals when:**

- Medical or legal consequences
- Diagnosis needed
- Crisis-level distress

1. Support reflection

3. Suggest appropriate human resource
4. Defer action to humans with accountability
